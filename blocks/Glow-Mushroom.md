A **glow mushroom** is a variety of fungus that grows and spreads in dark areas.
Glow mushrooms generate small or huge in size.

## Obtaining

### Breaking
Glow mushrooms can be instantly mined with anything. 
Mining a huge [glow mushroom block](./Glow-Mushroom-Block) drops up to two mushrooms of the same color.
Fortune does not affect the drop rate of mushrooms in any form.

A glow mushroom also breaks instantly and drops as an item if:
- the block below it is moved or destroyed 
- A fluid flows over it;
- The glow mushroom is not on podzol, mycelium, or nylium, the light level is 13 or higher, and a block updates next to it.

### Natural generation
Glow mushrooms naturally generate in [glow shroom caves](./Glow-Shroom-Caves) in form of small random vegetation patches on top of mycelium.

### Mob loot
Shearing a [glow mooshroom](./Glow-Mooshroom) drops 5 glow mushrooms and turns it into a normal cow.

## Usage
Glow mushrooms can be planted and grown on blocks in light levels below 13 and not directly underneath the sky.
If planted on mycelium, podzol or nylium, they can stay planted in any light level, including be planted and remain under light from sky above.

### Huge mushroom
When bone meal is applied to a glow mushroom, the mushroom has a chance to grow into a huge glow mushroom, provided that it is on a proper block (grass block, dirt, coarse dirt, rooted dirt or moss block at a light level of 12 or below; on podzol, mycelium or nylium at any light level) and has enough space (7x7x9).

### Spread
When receiving a random tick, a glow mushroom has a chance of spreading to a nearby block (including even above and below diagonally) if there are fewer than five mushrooms of the same type in a 9×9×3 volume around the original mushroom.

### Light source
Glow mushrooms emit a light level of 5.

### Decoration
Glow mushrooms can be planted in a flower pot.

### Composting
Placing a glow mushroom into a composter has a 65% chance of raising the compost level by 1.
